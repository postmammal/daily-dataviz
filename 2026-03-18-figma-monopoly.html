Figma's Share of the UI Design Tool Market — 2017 to 2026
Key Finding
No design tool has ever achieved market dominance this fast. Sketch peaked around 30% before Figma's cloud-first multiplayer approach made local-file tools feel obsolete overnight. The shift happened in just 4 years (2018–2022) — driven by remote work forcing teams to abandon desktop-only software.

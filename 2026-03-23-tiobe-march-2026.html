Python leads at 21% as C# records the biggest climb in the top ten
The TIOBE March 2026 index shows Python still commanding nearly twice the share of any rival — but below the summit, C surged past C++ and Java, and C# posted the largest year-on-year gain of any top-10 language, earning the 2025 Language of the Year award.

Key Finding

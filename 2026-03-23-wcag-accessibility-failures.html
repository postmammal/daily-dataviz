Key Finding
Low-contrast text is the single most prevalent failure — hitting 79.1% of pages at an average of 30 instances each — yet it's one of the easiest issues to fix with any modern color-contrast checker.
WCAG Failure Rate Trend — % of Home Pages Failing (2019–2025)
Key Finding
Six years of measurement show only 3.1 percentage points of improvement — from 97.8% failing in 2019 to 94.8% in 2025. At this pace, it would take until 2075 for even half of websites to become accessible.
The ARIA Paradox
Pages using ARIA average 57 errors — vs. 27 without it
ARIA (Accessible Rich Internet Applications) attributes are meant to improve accessibility, but pages deploying them show 2× more detected errors. This likely reflects more complex pages — but also developers reaching for ARIA before mastering the basics.

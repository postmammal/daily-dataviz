Design System Health — 2025 vs 2026 (Dumbbell Chart)
Key Finding
The 10-point drop in buy-in satisfaction reflects a familiar pattern: executives funded design system initiatives during the "Peak of Inflated Expectations," but when they didn't immediately solve every design consistency problem, support dried up. Teams are now stuck maintaining complex infrastructure without the resources to evolve it.

Key Finding
Design systems fail through under-documentation, not under-design. A system without onboarding is just a library nobody uses. The data shows 76% of teams provide no onboarding materials whatsoever — which explains why "adoption" remains the #1 complaint even for mature systems.

Design System Trust & Adoption Score by Age (normalized to 100)
Key Finding
The most important variable in design system success isn't the technology stack, the tooling, or even the team size — it's time. Systems older than 3 years score 12% higher on trust and significantly higher on adoption. The implication: the teams abandoning their systems before year 3 are killing them right before the payoff.

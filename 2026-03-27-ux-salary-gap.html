Key Finding
UX Researchers out-earn Designers by $25K at the junior level — a 26% premium that shrinks to just $9K (6%) at senior level, as designer ceiling catches up. UX Writers begin at $56K, a $64K gap from researchers, but their career trajectory shows 132% growth from junior to senior — the steepest arc of any UX role.

Key Finding
Despite the lowest entry salary, UX Writers see the most dramatic career trajectory — 132% growth from junior to senior, versus 37% for Researchers and 64% for Designers. Writers who reach senior level ($130K) close most of the gap, but the researcher-writer spread widens in absolute dollar terms as careers progress: $64K at entry, $35K at the top.

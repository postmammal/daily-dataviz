Market Map — Cost vs. Capability (bubble = relative developer adoption)
Key Finding
The market is bifurcating into two clusters: a dense budget tier (under $0.50/M) where most developers operate day-to-day, and a sparse frontier tier ($3–21/M) reserved for the highest-stakes tasks. The "missing middle" — mid-capability models at mid-prices — is rapidly being hollowed out as providers race to the extremes.
